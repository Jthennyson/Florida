FLORIDA FUN FACTS

  • Florida means "Land of Flowers" in Spanish.
  • Florida has the largest collection of Art Deco architecture in the world (Miami Beach).
  • Walt Disney World is the largest theme park in the world.
  • The Everglades is the only place where alligators and crocodiles coexist.
  • Florida has 1,350 miles of coastline, second only to Alaska.
