  1. Population

    Orlando has a population of approximately 290,000 people.

  2. Incorporation

    Orlando was incorporated in 1875.

  3. Region of Florida

    Orlando is located in Central Florida, a region famous for its tourism and theme parks.

  4. Classification

    Orlando is an urban city, driven largely by tourism, entertainment, and the tech industry.

  5. Average Income

    The average income in Orlando is around $50,000, roughly in line with the state average. However, given the area’s reliance on tourism, wages in the hospitality industry tend to be lower, while jobs in technology or corporate sectors tend to offer higher income.
