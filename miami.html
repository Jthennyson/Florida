  1. Population

    Miami has a population of approximately 470,000 people.

  2. Incorporation

    Miami was incorporated in 1896.

  3. Region of Florida

    Miami is located in the Southeast region of Florida, in the coastal area of the state.

  4. Classification

    Miami is a major metropolitan city, known for its international trade, tourism, and vibrant cultural scene.

  5. Average Income

    The average income in Miami is higher than the state average. The median household income is around $40,000; however, this varies greatly due to a significant income disparity across different neighborhoods.
