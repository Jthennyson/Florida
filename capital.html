  1. Population

    Tallahassee has a population of approximately 200,000 people

  2. Incorporation

    Tallahassee was incorporated in 1824

  3. Region of Florida

    Tallahassee is located in the Panhandle region of Florida, in the northern part of the state

  4. Classification

    Tallahassee is an urban city, serving as the political center of Florida with a significant governmental presence

  5. Average Income

    he average income in Tallahassee is generally lower than the state average due to its large student population and government sector economy. The median household income is around $50,000, which is below Florida’s overall median income of around $60,000
